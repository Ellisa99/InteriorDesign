Services and prices

LADesign design studio brings to your attention price packages for interior design. The price of interior design depends on the amount of work required to develop a perfect and correct design. When forming the cost of the service, we take into account the number of sketches, visualizations, drawings of engineering systems, as well as all your wishes.

When calculating the price of interior design, we take into account the entire list of works so that the design project can be implemented. Not all Customers know the real reason for such a variety of interior design prices. This is due to the fact that the designer can do the work superficially and not think over the implementation.

Our team gives a guarantee: the design project of the LADesign studio is not only perfect in design, it is feasible. We take into account all the pitfalls that in most cases appear after ordering an interior design.
